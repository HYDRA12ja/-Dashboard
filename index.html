🚀 Startup Company Dashboard

Web Design & Software Development Company Launch Plan

📌 Overview

This dashboard helps you track your startup progress from planning to client acquisition and scaling.

✅ Phase 1: Business Planning

  • Understand business model
  • Define services (Web, AI, Software)
  • Assign team roles
  • Choose specialization
  • Create pricing packages

🏢 Phase 2: Company Setup

  • Choose company name & branding
  • Register Pvt Ltd company
  • Open business bank account
  • Setup tools (Slack, Notion, GitHub)
  • Build company website

📈 Phase 3: Client Acquisition

  • Create prospect list (50 businesses)
  • Prepare sales pitch
  • Contact first leads
  • Secure first client
  • Build portfolio
